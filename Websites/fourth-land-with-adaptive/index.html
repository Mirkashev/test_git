NOW (2021)

I joined the AWWWARDS jury winning an honorable mention for one of my projects and usually I split my time building up design projects, developing both errands and personal websites.

I am a developer and web designer from Verona, Italy.

A web designer, as an architect in the construction of a building, must have skills ranging from the study of the single element to the complexity of the project having skills related to digital communication and usability.

My job includes being responsible for the technical functioning of the site, for the visual impact therein and for the user experience. A web designer is the ultimate responsible for the quality of a website and ensures that they are graphically appealing and easy to navigate, combining the needs of visitors and the accessibility.
