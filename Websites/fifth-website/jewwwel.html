Jewwwel AR App

WEBSITE & APP DESIGN

Idea

THE FOUNDERS OF THE COMPANY HAVE EXPERIENCE IN DEVELOPING AND LAUNCHING PRODUCTS IN THE JEWELRY INDUSTRY, COMBINED WITH DEVELOPMENT EXPERIENCE IN THE FIELD OF IMAGE RECOGNITION USING NEURAL NETWORKS AND CREATING AUGMENTED REALITY MOBILE APPLICATIONS.

PROJECT WAS STARTED IN SEPTEMBER 2019, AND IN A SHORT TIME, WITHOUT MAJOR INVESTMENTS, WE DEVELOPED A FULLY FUNCTIONAL DESKTOP PROTOTYPE OF THE PRODUCT. MOBILE APPLICATION IS CURRENTLY BEING DEVELOPED. WE BELIEVE THAT ALL JEWELRY COMPANIES, EVEN BRANDS WITH A LONG HISTORY, IN THE FUTURE WILL NEED TO STRENGTHEN THEIR ONLINE PRESENCE.
